Nasra Farah
-Author & Poet

Nasra Farah is an Ottawa-based Independent Author & Poet.
Poetry came into her life in her early teens,
as she would religously watch Def Poetry Jam performances
by, Amir Sulaiman, Thea Monyee, Daniel Beaty
and many more.
Inspired by spoken word, Nasra began writing poetry
as a way to reflect and cope with life.
Nasra published You and I, in June of 2019.
A collection of poems written
throughout her early teens
and reversed in her early 20s.
Nasra continues to write for her community, mental health
grief, love and feelings of diaspora.
