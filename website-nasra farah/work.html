You and I'
A book of poems dedicated to a lost love


You and I is a diagloue,
between the reader and parts of themselves.
Whether those parts significes
mental health, past memories and old relationships, faith,
or what it means to be a minority.

Nasra's summaries You and I, as a
'conversation with everything we hold close.'

You and I is divided into 8 chapters.
Each chapter connects to the next,
inspired by Nasra's personal experiences,
to the people that passed her, and those who decided to stay.
